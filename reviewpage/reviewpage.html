ABOUT

The space Our country house consists of a large kitchen - living room, two double bedrooms and one single bedroom and 2 bathrooms. Besides that, outside, there is a nice veranda and garden to enjoy and relax. The country house is located in Canicattì.It is only 20 min. from the sea and 2 min. by car from the center of Canicattì, where you will find bars, supermarkets, pizzerias, restaurants, bank, railway station etc. Canicattì is a town of 35 000 inhabitants 30 km from Agrigento, Caltanissetta to 30 km, 170 km and 180 km from Catania to Palermo. We provide on demand a special breakfast with aperitifs and varied, genuine local products as well as our homemade jams. Also on demand we can also serve you dinner. We provide wi-fi, parking spot, indoor fireplace and airconditioned ambient.

LOCATION

Kelly’s home is located in Brooklyn, New York, United States. Williamsburg is a vibrant and hip neighborhood in Brooklyn packed with incredible restaurants, boutiques, cafes, and bars. Several parks, beautiful street art, and stunning waterfront views of the Manhattan skyline make this the neighborhood to stay in for your visit to New York.

Getting around

We are only minutes walking distance from the J, L, M, and Z trains which are all only one stop away from Manhattan. There is free street parking right outside the building. A Citi bike station is one block away. The East River Ferry is about a 15 minute walk away as well!
